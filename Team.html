FC BARCELONA

Futbol Club Barcelona, commonly known as FC Barcelona and colloquially as Barça, is a professional football club based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain, that competes in La Liga.
Manager: Hansi Flick
Captain: Marc-André ter Stegen
Arena/Stadium: Lluís Companys Olympic Stadium
Founded: 29 November 1899
Leagues: LaLiga, UEFA Champions League, Copa del Rey
Nicknames: Barcelona, Barça, Blaugrana
Training ground: Ciutat Esportiva Joan Gamper.

ARGENTINA NATIONAL TEAM

The Argentina national football team, nicknamed La Albiceleste, represents Argentina in men's international football.
Captain: Lionel Messi
Manager: Lionel Scaloni
Arenas/Stadiums: Estadio Mâs Monumental, Estadio Mario Alberto Kempes
Leagues: World Cup, CONMEBOL-UEFA Cup of Champions
Location: Buenos Aires, Argentina

REAL MADRID C F

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, commonly referred to as Real Madrid, is a Spanish professional football club based in Madrid, Spain.
Manager: Carlo Ancelotti
Leagues: UEFA Champions League, LaLiga, Supercopa de España, Copa del Rey
Founded: 6 March 1902
Nicknames: La Casa Blanca (The White House), Los Blancos (The Whites)
Arena/Stadium: Santiago Bernabéu Stadium
Location: Madrid, Spain

INTER MIAMI CF

Inter Miami CF is an American professional football club based in Miami, Florida, that competes in Major League Soccer (MLS).
Manager: Gerardo Martino
Captain: Lionel Messi
Arena/Stadium: DRV PNK Stadium
Founded: 2018
Leagues: MLS, Leagues Cup
Nicknames: The Herons
Location: Miami, Florida, USA

Stadium: Al-Awwal Park
