FC BARCELONA VS VALLADOLID Recap

Raphinha's hat trick helped Barcelona to an imperious victory over Real Valladolid on Saturday. Raphinha and Robert Lewandowski gave the host a two-goal lead within four minutes with similar efforts from counterattacks, controlling long balls by Pau Cubarsí and Lamine Yamal before tidy finishes past the goalkeeper.

REAL MADRID CF VS ATLETICO DE MADRID Recap

Eder Militao scored early but Angel Correa equalised late on as Atletico claimed a dramatic draw in the Madrid derby on Sunday evening. The fiery contest had to be halted late in the second half due to the behaviour of some fans, with Thibaut Courtois having complained about objects being thrown in his direction from behind the goal. Carlo Ancelotti's side had to settle for a draw.

ARGENTINA VS FRANCE Recap

Argentina beat France 4-2 on penalties to win the 2022 World Cup after a 3-3 draw in a thrilling final at the Lusail Stadium in Doha, Qatar, on Sunday. Argentina raced into a two-goal lead through Lionel Messi and Angel Di Maria before two Kylian Mbappe goals saw holders France pull level in spectacular fashion.

NATIONS LEAGUE FINAL 2025 Recap

Portugal defeated Spain on penalties after a 2-2 draw in the UEFA Nations League Final 2025. Cristiano Ronaldo gave Portugal the equaliser with a stunning second-half strike. After extra time ended level, the match went to penalties where Portugal triumphed 4-3, lifting the trophy in dramatic fashion.

MANCHESTER CITY VS LIVERPOOL Recap

Manchester City edged Liverpool 2-1 in a thrilling Premier League clash at the Etihad Stadium. Erling Haaland opened the scoring with a powerful header, but Mohamed Salah equalised shortly after halftime. Kevin De Bruyne sealed the win late with a stunning long-range strike.

PSG VS BAYERN MUNICH Recap

Paris Saint-Germain stunned Bayern Munich with a 3-2 victory at the Parc des Princes. Kylian Mbappé scored twice, including a brilliant solo goal on the counter, while Neymar provided two assists. Harry Kane netted for Bayern, but PSG held on in a Champions League thriller.

CHELSEA VS ARSENAL Recap

Arsenal came from behind to beat Chelsea 3-1 at Stamford Bridge. Raheem Sterling gave Chelsea the lead, but goals from Bukayo Saka, Gabriel Jesus, and Martin Ødegaard turned the tide in favor of the Gunners, keeping their title hopes alive.
